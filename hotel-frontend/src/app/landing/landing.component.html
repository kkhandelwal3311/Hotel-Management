FAQs



What are the valid ID proofs that I should carry ?
You need to carry a valid government issued address ID proof with photo and address not in the same city as the hotel. Some of the valid id proofs are: Voter ID Card, Valid Passport, Aadhar Card issued by government of India, Permanent Driving License. PAN Cards are NOT Valid as ID Proofs
What are the standard check-in and check-out timings?
Our standard check-in time is 12 noon and check-out time is 11 am. However some properties may have different check-in and check-out schedule. You can view the same in the"House Rules" section in the Hotel listing page on website and just above the check-in and check-out dates in the Hotel listing page on the app. Early check-in and late check-out both are subject to room availability at the hotel.

What happens if I do not cancel my reservation and do not turn up at FabHotels on the arrival date?
Such a case is termed as No Show. In case of no show: Entire booking cost will be charged as cancellation fee.

Parking may be available at some of our hotels. For information about a specific hotel, please check the hotel information page for exact details.
